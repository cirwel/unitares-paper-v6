## EISV component discriminative power (last 30 days, fleet-aggregate)

`trajectory_validated` outcomes, n_good = 22,740, n_bad = 580.
Cohen's d, sign convention: positive d = bad outcomes have higher value.

| Component          | d      | Effect      |
|--------------------|--------|-------------|
| E                  | -0.542 | medium      |
| I                  | -0.289 | small       |
| S                  | +0.874 | large       |
| V                  | -0.326 | small       |
| phi                | -0.860 | large       |
| **legacy coherence** | **+0.133** | **negligible** |

Reading: the named state coordinates (especially S, phi, E) carry
meaningful predictive signal. The legacy `tanh`-of-V coherence — the
verdict-mapping function in deployment — does not. Means for legacy
coherence are 0.490 (good) vs 0.491 (bad), indistinguishable.

This is direct empirical support for the v6.x decision to ground
coherence on a manifold-distance formulation. The published
`tanh`-of-V form is the part that fails on its own audit log; the
state coordinates it consumes are not the failure.

| EXPLORATION | true   |   231 | 0.333  | 0.137    | 0.492      |
| TRANSITION  | false  |   654 | 0.271  | 0.180    | 0.488      |
| TRANSITION  | true   |    71 | 0.325  | 0.158    | 0.491      |

In CONVERGENCE, bad outcomes have *lower* S (premature lock-in on
wrong answer). In DIVERGENCE / EXPLORATION / TRANSITION, bad outcomes
have *higher* S (runaway uncertainty).

The directional sign of S → outcome is regime-dependent. A
fleet-wide S threshold would gate the wrong direction for one of the
regimes. **Class-conditional gating is what the data is asking for**,
which is exactly the structural move v6 introduces with class-keyed
healthy operating points.

Legacy coherence is flat (~0.49) across every regime × is_bad cell —
the discriminative power is uniformly absent, not concentrated in any
particular regime.

## Implication for v6.10 / v7

1. EISV state coordinates are load-bearing instrumentation. The
   antithesis claim that the four-dim cut is overhead is refuted by
   d=0.87 on S and d=0.86 on phi.
2. Legacy `tanh`-of-V coherence is not load-bearing. d=0.13 means
   verdicts derived from it are essentially random with respect to
   downstream outcomes. The class-conditional manifold-coherence
   reformulation is the empirically required replacement, not a
   theoretical preference.
3. The class-conditional thesis is empirically validated by the
   regime-inversion of S, available now in the audit log. v7 does not
   need to wait on Q3 2026 corpus maturity to argue the structural
   point — corpus maturity is needed for the *magnitude* of class
   constants, not for the *existence* of class-conditional structure.
